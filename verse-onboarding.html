Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

New to Verse? Here are the answers to the questions every newcomer asks.

What is Verse? +
Verse is a Web3 community that combines education, research, content creation, and marketing to help members build skills and discover opportunities in blockchain and decentralized technologies. We're a launchpad for anyone looking to enter or grow within the Web3 space.
How do I earn rewards? +
Rewards are earned by actively contributing to the community — completing tasks, joining campaigns, winning challenges, and participating in events. Connect your Polygon wallet on hub.vgdh.io to receive your rewards directly to your wallet.
How do I join events? +
Events are announced in the community channels. Keep an eye on the announcements section on Discord or Telegram, and RSVP when signups open. Most events are free for all members and some offer attendance rewards.
How do I participate in campaigns? +
Campaign opportunities are shared in dedicated channels. Once a campaign is live, follow the instructions provided — this usually involves completing tasks on partner platforms, then submitting proof of completion to qualify for rewards.
How do I connect my wallet? +
Visit hub.vgdh.io and sign in using your email, GitHub, or Discord account. Navigate to the Rewards section, click Connect Wallet, choose your Polygon wallet (MetaMask or similar), and sign the confirmation message. Your wallet is then linked to your Verse profile.
